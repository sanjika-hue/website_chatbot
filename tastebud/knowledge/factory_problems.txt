title: Machine Failure, Breakdown Prevention and Predictive Maintenance

Machine failure, unexpected breakdown, machines stopping suddenly, equipment failing, machines making weird sounds, strange vibrations — these are problems Hashtee solves with Obi. Obi listens to machines using a microphone and detects early signs of trouble — unusual sounds, vibrations, or wear — before a breakdown happens. It gives 24 to 72 hours of warning before a machine fails, so teams can schedule maintenance instead of dealing with a sudden stoppage. This prevents unplanned downtime, reduces emergency repair costs, and keeps production running smoothly. No sensors or wiring changes needed — just a microphone placed near the machine. Used at Maruti Suzuki to detect faulty engine components by sound alone, without opening the engine.
