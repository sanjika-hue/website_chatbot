title: Industries Hashtee Serves

Hashtee serves multiple industries with tailored AI solutions: Manufacturing: Obi (sound monitoring) + Dodo (quality inspection) + Binbin (WIP tracking). Clients: Maruti Suzuki, ITT, Mubea, Schneider Electric. Textile: Dodo (yarn/fabric defect detection) + Binbin (production flow tracking) + Longlichi (tag/label reading). Clients: Aditya Birla Grasim. Retail: Binbin (shopper tracking, path analysis) + Longlichi (tag reading, invoice processing) + Pechi (customer chatbot). Clients: Pantaloons, Aditya Birla Opus Paints. Paints/Chemicals: Longlichi (vendor document processing) + Pechi (customer queries). Clients: Aditya Birla Opus Paints. Automotive: Obi (engine noise detection) + Dodo (component inspection) + custom AI systems. Clients: Maruti Suzuki, Mubea. Real Estate and Construction: Dodo AR-based property inspection app. Inspectors place anchor grids and AI automatically detects broken tiles, cracks, and paint issues. Generates full quality checklist, 3D room rendering, work progress tracking, and paint area estimation.
